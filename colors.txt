### LIGHT THEME

background close to white
text close to black
subtext (text but darker/faded)

accent
accent text

button background (bg)
button hover    (essentially bg but lighter/darker)
button accent (based on accent)

### END LIGHT THEME

### DARK THEME
background similar to dark grey
text close to white (maybe with a tinge of accent or background)
subtext (text but darker/faded)

accent
accent text

button background (bg)
button hover    (essentially bg but lighter)
button accent (based on accent)
